01 — About

Telomeres are the protective caps on our chromosomes that shorten with every cell division. When they shrink below a certain threshold, the cell can no longer survive. They are one of the fundamental mechanisms of ageing, invisible clocks that never stop ticking.

When I first learned about them, I realized how precisely they capture the world around us. Everything changes and slowly fades — the color of flowers in a vase, the smoke from a blown-out candle, the scent of someone we love.

I have been making perfumes for over two years. What fascinates me the most about fragrances is elusiveness. Scents evolve, fade, and eventually disappear. They cannot be held or preserved. We must experience them while they're still here.

Telomeres is a brand about finding beauty in impermanence. Not about mourning the fact that things fade, but about the attention they deserve for the very reason that they will soon be gone.
