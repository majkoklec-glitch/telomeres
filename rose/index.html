Rose.

Rose was the first complete formula I decided to share. Built around rose, it makes no attempt to mimic the flower's natural scent. Instead, it captures something closer to the feeling of the color pink — delicate, luminous, and alive.

The opening reveals a juicy green apple accord, paired with a honey base containing beeswax absolute. Helvetolide plays a significant role — a musk with a fruity, radiant character that is perceptible from the start, giving the fragrance its airiness.

As the fruit softens, the heart arrives, where lavender essential oil meets Turkish rose absolute. A peony accord is also central, giving the composition its bloom. The florals persist all the way into the base, where they are softened by a creamy undertone of magnolia and sandalwood.

Rose feels light and airy, yet its longevity on skin is impressive.

Rose Parfum 50 ml

Composition

Top green apple, beeswax absolute, Helvetolide
Heart lavender, turkish rose absolute, peony accord
Base magnolia, sandalwood
