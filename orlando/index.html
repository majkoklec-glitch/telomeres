Orlando.

Orlando was born from the memory of a day spent in northern Sicily. It captures morning light over the city, the endless sea, and the cool touch of saltwater on sun-warmed skin — sharp, fresh and addictive.

The opening belongs to Sicilian mandarin, Sicilian bergamot, red orange and ginger. The citrus notes are bright and sunny, but at the heart the fragrance cools with a salty sea breeze and spearmint.

A base of white amber, musks and dry woods lends the whole composition softness and calm.

Orlando remains fresh, sun-drenched and marine. It lingers like a memory of a day already passed, but one we still carry within us.

Composition

Top sicilian mandarin, sicilian bergamot, red orange, ginger
Heart sea breeze, spearmint
Base white amber, musks, dry woods
