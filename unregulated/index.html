Unregulated.

Unregulated was born by accident. I was attempting to interpret a GCMS analysis of a Kilian fragrance, but the result felt unsatisfying. What stayed with me was the direction the composition had taken. After a few more adjustments, something interesting began to emerge.

The name Unregulated reflects its use of materials now banned in mainstream perfumery — primarily lilial and lyral. It is not a nostalgic return to fragrances of the last century, but rather a reflection of a time when perfumers worked with a greater freedom.

The composition contains many natural ingredients, with patchouli at the forefront. It opens with bergamot, cardamom and a lavender accord. After the initial freshness, the heart arrives — built around a nutmeg absolute, complemented by dark patchouli, tonka bean absolute and a lily of the valley accord.

In the base, a clean and pleasant trail of musk, vanilla and a gentle caramel nuance remains.

Unregulated Parfum 50 ml

Composition

Top bergamot, cardamom, lavender
Heart nutmeg absolute, dark patchouli, tonka bean absolute, lily of the valley
Base vanilla, musk, caramel
