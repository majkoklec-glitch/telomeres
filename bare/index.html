Bare.

Bare began as an interpretation of Prada's Cargo de Nuit but gradually drifted far enough to find its own character.

The composition is deliberately simple, yet remarkably clean. What makes it distinctive is that clarity is not built on aldehydes or typical soapy materials. Instead, it relies on subtle contrasts and unexpected details — like carrot seed oil.

Coumarin brings a soft, slightly sweet tone, recalling hay or marzipan. An iris accord and sandalwood add creaminess and delicacy. The result is a fragrance that evokes the feeling of clean skin and quiet elegance.

Bare Parfum 50 ml

Composition

Top carrot seed oil
Heart iris accord, coumarin
Base sandalwood
