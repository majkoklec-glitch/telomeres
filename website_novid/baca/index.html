Baca.

Baca is my most experimental creation. It began with an image of a green meadow where sheep are grazing — a fragrance that would be wearable, yet still raw and true to life.

The starting point was wool absolute, around which the entire composition gradually took shape. The aim was to find a balance between clean green tones, the dryness of hay, and a softly animalic character.

The opening unfolds in a green, grassy accord with hints of green apple and lavender. Through petitgrain and sage, the fragrance moves into the heart, where a prominent rhubarb accord emerges. The wool absolute remains central here, lending warmth, density, and a gentle animalic quality. A touch of blackcurrant bud absolute adds a subtle edge.

In the drydown, the fragrance turns towards a suede accord, darkened by styrax and patchouli. Lavender recedes, giving way to hay absolute — dry, dusty and faintly sweet.

Baca remains green, earthy and softly animalic to the very end. A scent of meadow, wool, hay, and skin after a day spent outdoors.

Baca Parfum 50 ml

Composition

Top green apple, lavender, petitgrain, sage
Heart rhubarb, wool absolute, blackcurrant bud absolute
Base suede accord, hay absolute, styrax, patchouli
