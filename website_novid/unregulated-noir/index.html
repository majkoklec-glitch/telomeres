Unregulated Night.

Unregulated Night takes the original character of the fragrance and pushes it into darker, richer territory. Where Unregulated is light and clean, Night is deep, dark and intense.

Cocoa absolute, coffee CO₂ extract, tobacco absolute, and an oud accord deepen the composition. They shift the fragrance closer to gourmand territory but without unnecessary sweetness. Instead, it feels velvety, dark, and warm, with a gently bitter undertone.

Night maintains its connection to the original Unregulated while revealing its darker side.

Unregulated Night · Parfum 50 ml

Composition

Top bergamot, cardamom, coffee CO₂
Heart nutmeg absolute, dark patchouli, cocoa absolute, tobacco absolute
Base vanilla, oud accord, musk
