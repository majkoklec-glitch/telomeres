Pulse.

Pulse is my take on the classic blue, citrus-fresh fragrance — clean, vibrant and full of energy. It builds on a bold citrus top of bergamot, lemon, orange and grapefruit.

Tension and originality emerge through pink pepper, ginger and elemi resin. Aquatic tones lend the citrus a wetter, more cooling dimension. Three types of aldehydes play a key role, especially mandarin aldehyde — a material rarely encountered in mainstream fragrances.

In the base, two forms of cedarwood shape the structure — an essential oil from Virginia and an absolute from the Atlas Mountains of Morocco. The absolute is rougher, earthier, less polished. The woods are complemented by a sandalwood accord, enriched with a touch of real Indian sandalwood.

Pulse is citrusy, aquatic, and clean. It carries the energy of a classic blue fragrance — yet with greater texture, depth, and character.

Pulse Parfum 50 ml

Composition

Top bergamot, lemon, orange, grapefruit
Heart pink pepper, ginger, elemi resin, aquatic notes, aldehydes
Base cedarwood (Virginia & Atlas), sandalwood accord, Indian sandalwood
