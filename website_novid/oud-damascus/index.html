Oud Damascus.

Oud Damascus is one of the most ambitious compositions I have created, and also among the most costly to produce. Its price reflects the exclusivity of the materials and the overall depth of the composition. It does not follow the classic structure of a clear opening and gradual development. Its character reveals itself from the very first moment.

At its heart lies a trio of raspberry, saffron and a Turkish rose absolute (Rosa damascena). However, the true core of the fragrance is natural oud oil from Cambodia. Used in generous proportion, it remains refined, without the harsh or animalic quality often associated with oud. Deep, dark and composed.

Patchouli, vanilla, amyris and Indian sandalwood bring softness and smoothness. A leather accord is present, though never smoky or raw. Instead, it merges seamlessly with the creaminess of the woods, gently darkened by styrax and guaiac wood.

The result is an opulent fragrance with remarkable longevity on skin, leaving a lasting, powerful trail in the air.

Oud Damascus Parfum 50 ml

Composition

Opening raspberry, saffron, turkish rose absolute
Heart natural oud oil (Cambodia), leather accord
Base patchouli, vanilla, amyris, Indian sandalwood, styrax, guaiac wood
