Cherry Promise.

Cherry Promise is a fragrance born from love. It began as a gift for my girlfriend, something meant only for her, but we soon realized it was too beautiful to keep to ourselves.

A creamy, gourmand perfume built around a vivid cherry accord — sweet, yet never cloying, like a tenderness that never overwhelms, an affection that never asks for too much. The cherry is paired with creamy sandalwood, a delicate touch of raspberry, and soft jasmine sambac absolute, lending the composition softness and fullness. A hint of almond and marzipan emerges from tonka bean absolute, bringing a quiet sense of completeness.

A trace of rhubarb in the opening brings gentle brightness, like the first spark between two people. In the base, vanilla softens and binds the composition, something that slowly settles into place.

Cherry Promise feels like a cherry suspended in a creamy haze. For some, it is a fruity gourmand; for others, a memory of cherry liqueur — but for me it is the scent of affection.

Cherry Promise Parfum 50 ml

Composition

Top rhubarb, cherry
Heart raspberry, jasmine sambac absolute, sandalwood accord
Base tonka bean absolute, vanilla
